# Dark Ages Equipment Kits - Occupation Mapping

Quick reference for assigning equipment kits to Dark Ages characters.

## Social Status Reflection

The kits reflect the social hierarchy of Dark Ages society:

**Elite Warriors** (WARRIOR KIT)
- Only those who can afford and maintain expensive equipment
- Chainmail requires constant maintenance
- War horses are status symbols

**Professional Combatants** (MERCENARY, GUARD KITS)
- More practical, maintainable armor
- Functional weapons over status weapons
- Field-ready gear

**Educated Classes** (SCHOLAR, CLERIC KITS)
- Investment in books and writing materials
- Staff for defense only
- Status from knowledge, not martial prowess

**Religious Poor** (MONK, PILGRIM KITS)
- Vow of poverty limits possessions
- Reliance on charity
- Spiritual rather than material wealth

**Commercial Class** (MERCHANT KIT)
- Investment in trade infrastructure
- Can afford quality goods but not military gear
- Wealth in goods and animals

**Working Class** (FARMER, CRAFTSPERSON KITS)
- Basic tools of trade
- Minimal defensive capability
- Focus on labor and survival

**Subsistence** (Applicable to multiple lower-status occupations)
- Minimal possessions
- Basic clothing and tools only
- Self-defense weapons only
